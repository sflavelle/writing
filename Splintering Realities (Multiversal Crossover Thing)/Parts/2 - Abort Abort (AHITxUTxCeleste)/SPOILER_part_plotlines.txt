Act 2 (AHIT/UT) Story Summary
Story begins with the end stage of the Undertale Genocide run, with Frisk (possessed by the spirit of Chara) fighting Sans in the Judgement Hall. It's unknown how long this endless dance has been happening
Simultaneously, Hat Kid and Mu, having been pulled through a violent tear in space/time into Undertale's universe, are now searching the Underground for a Time Rift that was detected
When Hat Kid finds it, behind a pillar in a corner of the Hall, she activates it, causing time to stop and rewind (temporarily overriding Frisk's own LOAD/RESET ability) to an earlier stage of the fight
Because the rewind wasn't initiated by Frisk/Chara, they stumble, and Sans is ready and strikes them, incapacitating them
Sans then cautiously goes after Hat Kid, thinking that she is working in some way with Frisk, but HK denies involvement
Soon after Sans eliminates Frisk, as Sans, Mu and HK talk, Flowey regains power over the timeline and RESETs Undertale's universe - but the fracturing multiverse plus the addition of Mu and HK from their universe causes things to alter slightly, with characters such as Toriel remembering the past genocide timeline
With the reset, Frisk is no longer burdened with Chara, and they feel great remorse for their actions. Toriel intervenes sooner despite the memory of her accidental death, preventing Flowey from interacting with Frisk, Mu and HK first
The effects of the multiversal collision strike the Underground, causing earthquakes and dimensional instability - for example the surface of Mount Ebott merges with that of Mount Celeste (Celeste), bringing Madeline and Theo (and Part of Madeline) with it
With the reset, Sans also decides to intervene to prevent another genocide, meeting with Toriel at her house
Sans finds out that Toriel has retained some knowledge from the previous timeline
There is a general meeting between the kids, Sans, Toriel and Theo in which they introduce themselves, with Flowey eavesdropping
...Which is soon interrupted by Papyrus arriving through the open basement door, looking for Sans to warn that Snowdin is under attack!
It turns out, Moonjumper has already also arrived here and is laying siege to the Underground, with the vengeful spirit of Chara assisting in the slaughter in a more tangible form thanks to Moonjumper
As this is happening, and the gang is heading there as quick as possible to assist, Madeline is wandering around the Waterfall area, and eventually runs into Undyne, who gives chase for her soul
She chases her into Snowdin from the other end of town, where they see the destruction occurring, and the two agree to a truce in order to fend off the attack and get monsters to safety
Both groups split into teams, some fighting the two attackers and others rescuing monsters from the line of fire, or burning buildings
Namely, Toriel, Sans, Undyne, Mu and Flowey are fighting, while Hat Kid, Frisk and Madeline are helping
In the midst of the battle, Chara manages to get to Papyrus, and quickly dusts him in front of his brother Sans
Eventually Moonjumper halts the battle and declares his intent to wipe out the multiverse with Chara's help. He summons Alex (the 'player' protagonist from Act 1) and proceeds to publically, physically rip out her soul and toss it into the snow, Chara taking over the body and specifically calling out Flowey as Asriel, and both vanish to cause more havoc throughout the Underground
In the aftermath of the battle, the inhabitants are despondent, most gathered at a campfire while a plan is being organised. Hat Kid, Mu and Flowey are investigating Alex's soul, which has taken on the appearance of one of Undertale's heart-shaped SOULs (as they are within that universe currently), but is empty, only a fraction of the soul actually 'filled'. Sans, Frisk and Toriel are reconciling over the events of the previous timeline in the burned down Grillby's. Undyne, Madeline, 'Part of Her' and Theo converse (with input from the other monsters) at the campfire about magic and just what the hell is going on around here?
Eventually, a message gets through from the Capital, New Home: All survivors are to report to the Castle for an emergency briefing. That includes the humans/aliens
So eventually everyone gets to the Castle, and the outsiders must pass Judgement by Sans before entering the throne room where everyone else (most of the main cast of UT, plus a few other lucky monsters) has gathered
Sans is by now wearing Papyrus' scarf around his neck
Frisk passes this time, having been unable to begin a genocide and in fact having assisted in the defense of Snowdin
Theo and Madeline of course pass
Hat Kid is found to have an LV of 4, and Sans is hesitant, but she also assisted in the defense and has otherwise been helpful, so she passes
Mu passes with LV 1, but Sans makes a note of her flavor text, which implies danger
Asgore commences the emergency briefing, with Captain Undyne, Gerson, and Alphys giving accounts of the events occuring across Snowdin, Waterfall and Hotland respectively. Undyne vouches for the humans' help in the defense
Flowey publically notes that the secondary enemy was Chara, ending up needing to give evidence that ties him to his previous life as Asriel
Then Hat Kid and Mu are given an opportunity to explain what is happening, who Moonjumper is, that they are not from this universe, and asking for volunteers to help in their chase, travelling the multiverse
During this explanation, Snatcher arrives, having finally found them through Alex's soul signature, of which he already has a part from her earlier 'soul contact' in Act 1
Frisk volunteers immediately, feeling the need to atone for their previous actions
Flowey volunteers, wanting payback for Chara's betrayal
Sans eventually volunteers as well, under the guise of 'well someone needs to keep an eyesocket on these two' but also wanting payback for Papyrus' death
He also has something that he thinks could be beneficial - he believes his nonfunctional basement machine, from his scientist days, may in fact find a purpose on this adventure
The new crew return to Waterfall, beneath the surface opening that Madeline's crash-landing created, where with line-of-sight to the sky, Hat Kid beams everyone onto the spaceship
After a tour, Sans gets down to business, passing Judgement again before deciding if he will commit to this venture - this time scrutinising over their LVs and flavor text
To reiterate: Hat Kid has LV 4 but a generally positive flavor text; Mustache Girl (Mu) has LV 1 but the flavor text alludes to her role in AHIT
HK uses a Time Piece to show everyone there her adventure and the reasons for her LV, and realises that her actions on the Arctic Cruise had consequences, and debating over whether fulfilling the Fire Spirits' wishes to be consumed in the magical flames of Subcon should have counted toward her LV or not (LV/EXP does not care about assisted suicide, ultimately, even if monsters like Sans might be sympathetic to the reasoning)
After some debate, Sans accepts and decides to tag along, requiring only one more trip down to the Underground to fetch a few things - the machine, the trash tornado from his room, and also making a grave for Papyrus ('the coolest skeleton ever')
The final chapter is about the monsters' and Frisk's adjustment to life in space, and the calibration/integration of the machine to interface with the spaceship
