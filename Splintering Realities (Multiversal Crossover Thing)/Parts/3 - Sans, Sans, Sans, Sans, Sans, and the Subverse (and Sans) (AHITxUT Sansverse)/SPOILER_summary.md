Act 3 (UTMV) Plot Summary

- Sans' machine finally works! It's picked up Moonjumper's signal in a neighboring universe and managed to transport the team's spaceship there
- The ship appears in a void, something that Hat Kid has not experienced - but the signal guides the ship to a reality bubble, within which lies a castle (Nightmare's castle, the 'home base' of the group generally known as 'the Bad Sanses'
- When they land in the gardens, they are quickly met by someone who looks *just like Sans* - but in vastly different clothing, giving a semi-military vibe. This skeleton calls themselves Cross, and tells the crew they have arrived at a bad time, even after explaining they are chasing the Moonjumper (and are they working with him? Cross has no idea who they mean)
- They are led into the main living room of the castle where a battle between many different Sanses has broken out. Hat Kid intervenes with her Time Stop Hat to prevent a sudden attack on her group
- It turns out, they are fighting because the Guardians of Feelings, Dream and Nightmare, have both been slain suddenly, despite their functional immortality, and each group (The Star Sanses, Ink and Blue; and the Bad Sanses, Killer, Horror, Dust and Cross. Red is also there but he's sort of a neutral party) suspects the other. Our crew suspects Moonjumper, and tries to figure out why
- (The slaying is a means to acquire their power, with the added side-effect of destabilising this odd universe as a whole. Moonjumper is also targeting Error, for his universal-destruction abilities, but nobody knows this right now)
- During this, a duplicate Sans appears, the 'Classic' Sans each skeleton was referring to our Sans as. Hat Kid finally gets that this is a specific type of universe called a 'subverse', but refuses to elaborate
- After a reluctant ceasefire, Hat Kid, Frisk and Flowey are guided to Nightmare's office, the resting place of what is left of him, to do detective work. Mu, Snatcher and Sans check out the Star Sanses' base where Dream had been killed in his sleep
- The current consensus, after talking with everyone is: there is no reason that any of them would break a long-standing oath to refrain from attacking each other's bases directly, especially when each groups never knew where the other resided until they were brought together with Error's help - and even if Error sees all of them as 'glitches', 'anomalies' - he's still made acquaintances with the different Sanses and essentially 'tolerates' them
- That night, as the team sleeps (Snatcher pulled into a magically-induced sleep), both Dream and Nightmare introduce themselves, only able to communicate through the dreamscape. They explain their roles, and their current state (they exist as spirits, only able to communicate and exist in others' dreams), and learn more about the entity that slew them
- The next day, while plans are being made amongst our team and the skeletons, an apparent battle between Error and Moonjumper appears in the castle, and everyone leaps in to assist. Blue (Error) and red (Moonjumper) strings are everywhere, occasionally tying characters up. Ink is slain in this encounter, but the group successfully manages to beat Moonjumper back
- As thanks, Error and Classic take our crew to the 'Classic' Undertale 'verse within this subverse, where the Barrier was broken and Monsters are two years into living on the surface. In this reality the main cast bought out an apartment building and live together, using the ground floor as a shared space. When Hat Kid's crew arrive, they are met by (among other monsters) DJ Grooves the moon penguin from HK's universe, as well as a strange humanoid creature (Lily, the inkling from Begin Again)
